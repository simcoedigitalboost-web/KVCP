About KCVP

Meet Kel

The person behind the work — and why doing it right the first time isn't a slogan, it's the only way we operate.

KCVP Inc was built on a simple idea: homeowners deserve a contractor who shows up when they say they will, does the work right, and charges a fair price for it. Kel started KCVP after years of working in the trades and seeing the same problems over and over — sloppy work, inflated quotes, and contractors who disappear when something goes wrong.

As a licensed plumber with extensive hardscaping and excavation experience, Kel brings a rare combination of skills to every project. Most plumbers don't touch outdoor work. Most landscapers don't understand plumbing. KCVP does both — and that means better results for homeowners who need drainage, waterproofing, and outdoor spaces that actually work together.

Based in Barrie and serving communities across Simcoe County, KCVP handles everything from 3am emergency calls to summer patio builds. Every project gets the same standard of work — clean jobsites, honest communication, and results that hold up through Ontario's toughest seasons.
