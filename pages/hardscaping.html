Outdoor Living

Hardscaping

Your outdoor space should feel like an extension of your home — not a liability. We design and build patios, walkways, retaining walls, and steps using materials chosen specifically for Simcoe County's freeze-thaw cycles.

Because we also handle plumbing and drainage, we build hardscape projects with proper water management from the start. That means no pooling water on your patio, no retaining walls undermined by poor drainage, and no settling from inadequate base preparation.

  • Interlocking stone patios and walkways
  • Natural stone installations
  • Retaining walls (armour stone, segmental block, natural stone)
  • Front entrance steps and landings
  • Fire pit areas and outdoor living spaces
  • Garden bed edging and borders
  • Driveway extensions and aprons
Water Management

Drainage & Grading

Water goes somewhere — the question is whether it goes where you want it to. Poor drainage destroys foundations, floods basements, kills lawns, and undermines hardscaping. We fix it at the source.

As licensed plumbers with extensive excavation experience, we bring a level of expertise to drainage work that most landscapers simply can't match. We understand both the surface water and the subsurface plumbing systems that need to work together.

  • French drain installation
  • Weeping tile repair and replacement
  • Lot grading and regrading
  • Catch basins and channel drains
  • Downspout extension and discharge
  • Stormwater management systems
  • Swale construction
  • Window well drains
Foundation Protection

Waterproofing

A wet basement isn't just an inconvenience — it's a structural risk and a health hazard. We provide complete waterproofing solutions from the inside out, addressing both the symptoms and the root cause of water intrusion.

Our approach combines plumbing expertise with excavation know-how to deliver waterproofing that actually works long-term, not just a temporary patch that fails after the next spring thaw.

  • Exterior foundation waterproofing membrane
  • Interior waterproofing systems
  • Sump pump installation and replacement
  • Battery backup sump systems
  • Foundation crack repair
  • Basement leak diagnosis and repair
  • Window well waterproofing
  • Crawl space encapsulation
