24/7 Response

Emergency Plumbing

When water is pouring into your basement at 3am, the last thing you need is a voicemail. We answer the phone, day or night, weekends and holidays. Our emergency response covers Barrie and surrounding Simcoe County communities.

We arrive fast, assess the situation honestly, and fix the problem — not just patch it. You'll know the cost before we start, and we don't charge for showing up and telling you it's a simple fix.

  • Burst and frozen pipe repair
  • Sewage backup and overflow
  • Basement flooding and water damage mitigation
  • Gas leak detection and repair
  • No-heat water heater failures
  • Sump pump failure
  • Main water line breaks
  • Emergency drain clearing
